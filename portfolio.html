Portfolio

Portfolio of my semester projects at the Medialogy Bachelor at AAU cph campus.

2021-2024

Project 1

Waste sorting game for 2. graders

Theme:

FN’s 13th goal, Climate action, Waste sorting.

Abstract 1

This report focuses on the potential for a game to help the younger generation understand more about waste sorting. Based on extensive research surrounding waste sorting, global warming, alternative solutions and our target group, a tablet game was created. The functionality and teaching ability of our game was tested through a semi-structured interview method followed by a data-analysis on fellow students and one child for the expert test. The results showed an overall positive opinion on the game's design and understanding of the game's intention. Though, the game’s mechanics were mostly criticized. Discussion of improvements, solutions and future work of the project explains how the test should have been conducted more appropriately to gain usable results, and to later on improve upon the mechanics of the game.
